## Recommendation

**Default pool size: 10000 docs.**

At size 10000, between-seed nDCG@10 stddev falls below the 0.005 threshold (measured 0.0031), meaning pool-to-pool variance is smaller than typical improvement signals. Smaller sizes may work for stability/speed-only checks but are not reliable for detecting metric regressions, and deterministic undersized pools must be excluded from this decision.

Update the `/benchmark` skill's Phase 1 corpus-check table: `miracl-ko --size 10000`.
